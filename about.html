AKINSOLA DANIEL was born in the city of Lagos, His love for Art in a visual representation withe pencil on paper grew tremendously. Having grown up both in Lagos and in close proximity to Ado odo ota LG. In ogun state. Throughout his life, Daniel has been fascinated by images especially in comics book like spider man, superman and the likes. It's makes him grew as a lover of comics toons. naturally, he had passion for art . The interest was cultivated during during his teenage years and further develop when he opted to take his OND in Fine art at Yabatech and Bachelor Degree in Fine Art at Uniben He has trained to fit in the industrial studen work when he was in Yabatech, which has gave him more opportunities to work with professional on the site,It's a collaboration between artist and atholoc church art and had improve his Art carrier, He did more of painting and less suclptor during this process. He is a lover of all types of visual representation of Art, His paintings are just at the intersection of expression and realism.
